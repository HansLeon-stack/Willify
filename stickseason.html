Stick Season
Noah Kahan
"Stick Season" by Noah Kahan is a song that captures the essence of seasonal change and personal reflection. The title refers to a transitional period in New England between fall and winter when the trees are bare, symbolizing a time of emptiness and anticipation. The lyrics delve into themes of loss, longing, and the passage of time, painting a vivid picture of rural life and the emotional landscape that comes with it.
