Popular
The Weeknd
"Popular" by The Weeknd, Playboi Carti, and Madonna is a vibrant pop track that fuses the distinctive styles of its collaborators. The song features catchy hooks, a pulsating beat, and a blend of nostalgic and contemporary pop elements. Lyrically, "Popular" delves into themes of fame, allure, and the complexities of being in the spotlight. The Weeknd's smooth vocals, Playboi Carti's energetic verses, and Madonna's iconic presence combine to create a dynamic and memorable song that has captured the attention of listeners in 2024.
