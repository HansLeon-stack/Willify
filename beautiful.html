Beautiful Things
Benson Boone
"Beautiful Things" by Benson Boone is a poignant and heartfelt song that explores themes of love, loss, and hope. The lyrics paint a vivid picture of finding beauty and meaning in life, even amidst challenges and heartache. Benson Boone's emotive voice brings depth to the song, conveying a sense of longing and appreciation for the simple, beautiful moments that often go unnoticed. The song reflects on the transformative power of love and the ways it can change one's perspective, making even the smallest things appear beautiful.
