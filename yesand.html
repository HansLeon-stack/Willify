Yes And
Ariana Grande
"Yes And" by Ariana Grande is an empowering anthem that celebrates the art of positivity and embracing life's possibilities. The song's title, inspired by the improv comedy principle of accepting and building on what is given, reflects a mindset of openness and growth. In "Yes And," Ariana Grande's signature powerful vocals shine as she sings about overcoming challenges and seizing opportunities. The lyrics emphasize resilience, adaptability, and the importance of saying "yes" to new experiences while also adding one's unique touch to them.
